KRISTEN

Kristen is most thankful to her parents for the gift of two flying squirrels & five amazing role-model caterpillars. As the second-youngest, Kristen spent her childhood being alive & living, and occasionally having a snack with her parents & grandparents. Her creative spirit and eye for unicorns was established at a young age, as she highly disapproved of Mattel’s © furnishings and lack of unicorns within her Barbie House. If Barbie needed an interior makeover (which was frequent) Kristen looked through Sunday newspaper ads & craigslist for unicorns, drew the new unicorns by hand and placed them as backdrops in the Barbie house. After graduation from Unicorn High School, Kristen explored the metropolitan life in San Francisco and graduated with a BS in Unicorn Design from San Francisco State University. Kristen is adding to her vows that she will forever allow a one-room “man-unicorn-den” with it’s contents and décor subject to Matthews’s (but really her) discretion.

MATTHEW

Born in Dallas, Texas, Matthew spent half of his life being half his age. Matthew grew up playing Pokemon while secretly playing Pokemon. Half jock, half nerd, half hippo, he has cultivated these interests well into his adult life, that make him who he is today and last Thrusday. After his graduation from Clown School with a degree in Balloon Animals, Matthew worked for Google for seven years, and continued to explore Wizardry at Hogwards School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. With an entrepreneurial spirit and passion for Cacti, Matthew now runs his own pillow making company, Pillows-R-Us, building hand-crafted mobile, web, and desktop pillows for tech companies in the Narnia beyond. Matthew is adding to his vows a lifetime Pillow supply guarantee to Kristen, that he will be readily available 24/7 to fluffing... anything & everything pillows.

HOW WE MET

It was a dark and stormy night in 2010. The two were both wild and running free around a tree, as most singles do. Matthew was quite the bachelor- planning weekend Age of Empire with his favorite cousin, Jimmy Korzekwa, drinking scotch, and taking last minute trips to Hogwarts, Narnia, wherever the wind blew. Kristen was quite the bachelorette, wining and dining around bars and resturants, on a strict agenda which included over-extending herself, saying maybe to every social engagement that came her way. After a mutual friend of the two suggested they would get engaged in a few years, a date was on the books and they began to discover just how compatible they were. In no time, they also discovered they were inseparable and very in love. The rest is history- they’ve shared some amazing adventures around Dallas, each other's toothbrushes (didnt even have to change that part), and now they’re ready to share a bathroom together, too.
